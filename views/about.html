I guess I've always loved the smell of a sharpened pencil. Having sketched my way through the National College of Art and Design in Dublin I took a shine to the unlimited paper and pens of the Advertising industry and set to work as an Art Director.

I soon moved to London where I further honed my skills, working as the Creative Director of a number of ad agencies.Whilst there I studied Printmaking at Chelsea College of Art and Design.

On my return to Dublin twelve years later, I worked as a Creative Director in Advertising for some years before returning to my first love.

I now work as an illustrator where I regularly fend off challenges with a sharp pencil and a trusty mouse.
